About us

Welcome to Peak Eclat

I am delighted to welcome you all to the Peak Éclat Junior School website. A platform that is meant to promote an excellent relationship and effective communication amongst all the stakeholders of our school.

As Peak Éclat Junior School, we are thrilled to have an opportunity to play in the education sector of this nation. We take pride in grooming well rounded personalities who are able to take the world in their stride.

Peak Éclat Junior School aims at maintaining the required equilibrium between mental capabilities, social and emotional intelligence.

We call upon all stakeholders in the education system to cooperate with us as we strive to achieve our vision of grooming an all-round citizen with traditional exposure that enhances both national and international morals.

We care we share for the future we prepare

HAJJ LUBEGA MUHAMMAD DIRECTOR.
